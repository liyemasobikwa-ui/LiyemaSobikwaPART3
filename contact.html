Frequently Asked Questions

❓ How do I measure my head for a wig?

Check our size guide on the Wigs page. Or send us a message and we will help you measure correctly.

❓ How do I take care of my wig?

Synthetic wigs: Wash every 6-8 wears with gentle shampoo. Air dry on a wig stand.
Human hair wigs: Treat like your own hair. Use good shampoo and conditioner. Store on a wig stand when not in use.

❓ Can I order a custom wig?

Yes! We make custom wigs for your specific needs. Tell us your color, length, and style. Use our enquiry form to start the process.

❓ What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, credit cards, and cash for local pickup.

❓ Do you ship across South Africa?

Yes, we ship to all provinces. Shipping cost depends on your location. Ask us for a quote.

❓ Can I return or exchange a wig?

For hygiene reasons, we cannot take back wigs that have been worn. If your wig is unused and in original packaging, you can exchange it within 7 days.

❓ Do you have a physical shop?

We are currently online only. Local pickup is available by appointment. Contact us to arrange a pickup time.
