Proposed Website Features and Functionality 

The Crown & Glory Wigs website will have several important pages that are intended to improve user experience and foster company expansion. A welcome message, call-to-action buttons, highlighted products, and a picture of a model in a wig to grab attention will all be included on the home page. The founder's biography, the company's mission, vision, and narrative will all be included on the About Us page. A gallery of wigs with pictures, types (such lace front, full lace, synthetic, and human hair), colors, and pricing will be shown on the Products page. Customers can submit purchasing questions and special orders using the form on the Enquiry page. Then the contact page will provide important information like phone number, email address, a WhatsApp link, and a FAQ section. 

Design and User Experience 

Crown & Glory Wigs will employ simple yet elegant colors, such as dark grey for easy-to-read text, pink for little details, white for the backdrop, and purple for headers and buttons. Poppins will be used for the body text since it is readable and clear, while Playfair Display will be used for the headings for a fashionable appearance. With a main image on the site and a product gallery where each wig is displayed in its own card with a picture, type, and price, the design will be straightforward and uncomplicated. With a card-based gallery that displays each wig image, style, and cost, the design will be straightforward and uncomplicated, emphasizing wig photos. With large "Enquire Now" buttons, obvious call-to-action phrases like "Shop Our Collection," visible WhatsApp contact information, simple navigation, and a FAQ section for frequently asked inquiries are all features. To create a modern, premium look, soft, rounded corners and light shadows will be used.

Organization Overview: 

Mzansi Youth Hub is a hypothetical non-profit organization headquartered in Katlehong, Gauteng. It was created in 2021 by a group of local university students who intended to solve young unemployment and school dropout rates. The organization’s purpose is to equip unemployed adolescents with free digital skills training, career coaching, and mental health support. Its objective is to create a South Africa where every young person has access to opportunities regardless of their background. The target audience comprises unemployed adolescents aged 18 to 29, high school pupils, and local community leaders. 

Website Goals and Objectives: 

The primary purpose of the website is to boost youth enrolment in free training programmes by 50% within six months. A secondary goal is to acquire monthly donations and business sponsors to support operations. Key performance metrics will include the number of online enrolment forms submitted, monthly donation amounts, and volunteer sign-ups monitored through the enquiry page. 

Current Website Analysis: 

At the moment, the organization doesn't have a website and only uses word-of-mouth and WhatsApp groups. Because of this flaw, many young people are unaware of the options that are out there. This will be resolved by the proposed website, which would establish a central, easily accessible hub where users can apply for programs, find course details, and make online donations. 

Features and Functionality: 

The website will include five pages: a homepage with success stories, an about us page explaining the organization’s impact, a programmes page listing free courses, an enquiry page for volunteer and sponsor applications, and a contact page with two physical locations (Katlehong and Vosloorus) displayed on embedded maps.
